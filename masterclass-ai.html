The Gap Between Ideas and Execution

The Problem

You know AI is important, but the path to using it for executing your own ideas isn't clear.

The tech field might feel overwhelming, and it can seem like you only have three options:

Use "off-the-shelf" tools that don't fit your specific needs.

Face a mountain of complex code that takes months to wrap your head around.

Hire developers who cost you $1,000s to build your idea.

The Solution

But there's another way. You can start forming your own army of AI-powered tools.

This masterclass gives you the "lego blocks" of building with AI (the essential 20% skills that unlock 80% of your ideas) plus mentoring to help you along the way.

You'll learn by doing and finish with real, working tools you built yourself—from a custom marketing copy generator that saves you hours each week, to an automated lead qualifier for smarter, data-driven decisions.
