## Laws and regulations researched

### GDPR (EU Regulation 2016/679)
- **Sources:** gdpr-info.eu, edpb.europa.eu
- **Findings:** Contact form and waitlist process personal data. Legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) chosen per Option A. Retention periods documented. User rights (access, rectification, erasure, etc.) apply.

### Dutch Handelsregisterwet
- **Sources:** kvk.nl, government.nl
- **Findings:** Companies must display legal name, address, KvK number on website. Implemented in footer.

### Brevo (Sendinblue)
- **Sources:** brevo.com/legal, help.brevo.com
- **Findings:** EEA-based (France). GDPR compliant. DPA available. 72h breach notification commitment. User rights support (rectification, erasure, portability).

### Google Cloud / Sheets API
- **Sources:** cloud.google.com/terms
- **Findings:** Cloud Data Processing Addendum (CDPA) available. SCCs for international transfers. Subprocessor list published.

### ePrivacy / Cookies
- **Sources:** EDPB, commission.europa.eu
- **Findings:** No analytics or marketing cookies on site. Server logs only. Cookie disclosure added to Privacy Policy. No consent banner required for current setup.

### EAA (EU Accessibility Act)
- **Sources:** EU directive, implementation 28 June 2025
- **Findings:** Micro-enterprise exemption may apply. Documented in pre-launch checklist for future assessment.
