Python Generative AI Web Scraping Data Analytics Reddit API
The Story Behind MarketSignal

As a digital nomad constantly exploring new markets and opportunities, I noticed how valuable Reddit discussions were for understanding user needs and market trends. However, manually analyzing thousands of posts was time-consuming and inefficient. This led me to build MarketSignal - an AI-powered tool that automatically processes Reddit discussions to extract actionable insights about user pain points, emerging opportunities, and competitive intelligence.

The project combines web scraping, natural language processing, and behavioral science principles to identify patterns that human analysts might miss. It's been used by entrepreneurs, product managers, and market researchers to make data-driven decisions about product development and market entry strategies.
